Force Recheck a Torrent

Torrenter automatically checks each piece of a torrent when necessary. However, you can force Torrenter to check the pieces whenever desired.

There is one way to do it:

  • Torrents table context menu

    • Right click on the torrent in the torrents table

    • Click Force Recheck in the context menu
